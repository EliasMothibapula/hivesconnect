# hivesconnect
DEMO site for SMEs Marketplace (does not include sign up and registration) shows how customer and sme screens will look
## About

Hives Connect is a comprehensive service marketplace platform demonstrating a complete ecosystem for connecting customers with SMEs and service providers in Botswana. The platform showcases secure escrow-based transactions, seamless booking workflows, and rich analytics dashboards for both parties.

## Key Features

**For Customers:**
- Discover services across Plumbing, Bakery, Electrical, Cleaning, and Repair categories
- View detailed provider profiles with work portfolios and reviews
- Book services with date/time selection, urgency levels, and photo attachments
- Secure escrow payments (money held until service completion)
- Negotiate pricing with SMEs through price suggestions
- Track bookings with 10-minute cancellation window
- Rate providers and leave detailed reviews
- Personal analytics showing spending patterns and favorite services

**For SMEs:**
- Accept, decline, or respond to service requests
- Suggest additional pricing with customer comments
- Confirm service completion to receive payment (95% to SME, 5% platform fee)
- Customer insights including retention metrics and job history
- Performance analytics covering earnings, completion rates, and customer satisfaction

**Platform:**
- Demo wallets: Customer P1000, SME P200
- Transaction history with full audit trail
- 40+ analytics metrics for both customers and SMEs
- Professional UI with Botswana context (Pula currency, local services)
- Mobile-responsive design

## Technology

- **HTML5**, **CSS3**, **JavaScript (ES6)**
- No external dependencies beyond Font Awesome and Google Fonts
- Complete frontend application with state management
